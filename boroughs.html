Bagels Across the City

Bagel culture exists in every borough of New York City, but each area has its own feel. Manhattan has many classic and famous bagel spots, Brooklyn has neighborhood favorites and trendy shops, and Queens also has strong local options. This shows that bagels are not just a tourist food. They are part of daily life in many different parts of the city.
