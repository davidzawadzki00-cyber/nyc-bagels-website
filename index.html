Welcome

New York City bagels are one of the most iconic foods in the city. They are known for their chewy texture, crisp outside, and wide variety of flavors and toppings. Whether someone likes a simple toasted bagel with cream cheese or a full bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich, NYC bagels are an important part of local food culture. This website explores the history, styles, sandwiches, and famous shops that make New York bagels special.
