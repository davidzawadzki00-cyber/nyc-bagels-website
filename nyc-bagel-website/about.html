Why NYC Bagels?

I chose NYC bagels for this website because they are one of the most recognizable foods in New York City and an important part of its everyday culture. Bagels are simple, but they also have a long history and many different styles, flavors, and serving options. This topic works well for a website because it includes strong visuals, interesting information, and many ways to organize content into different pages.
