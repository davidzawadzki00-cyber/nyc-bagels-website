Images of NYC Bagels

This gallery shows different examples of NYC bagels, including plain bagels, everything bagels, breakfast sandwiches, and bakery displays. Images are an important part of this topic because bagels are a food that people often judge by appearance, texture, and presentation. The photos help make the website more interesting and give visitors a better idea of what makes New York bagels stand out.
