More Than Cream Cheese

One reason NYC bagels are so popular is because they can be turned into great sandwiches. Some of the most common are bacon, egg, and cheese; lox with cream cheese; and turkey with egg whites or avocado. A bagel sandwich can be quick, filling, and customizable. In New York, many people treat bagels as both a breakfast food and an everyday meal.
